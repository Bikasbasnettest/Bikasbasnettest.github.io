Research Endowment Fund

The fund supports innovative projects and academic research in Nepal, fostering scientific advancement and community development. Contributions sustain cutting-edge studies, including:

  • Diversity study of flora and fauna (phenotypic and genomic scale)
  • Habitat conservation efforts
  • Climate-resilient ideas and innovation promotion
  • Location-specific innovations for farmers, like mechanization, machine learning, model deployment
  • Location-specific Quality seed and input timely supply
  • Chain and functional policy support and Development of farmers' products in different parts of the country

Key Interests

  • Genomics & Plant Breeding
  • Plant-Microbe Interactions
  • Quantitative Genetics
  • Computational Biology, Data Science & Machine Learning
  • Crop Science
Google Scholar ORCID

I am an enthusiastic graduate student in agriculture with avidity for not only crop improvement (genomics, plant-microbe interactions, quantitative genetics, applied aspects of plant breeding, modern plant breeding tools, product design and development), but also computational genetics programming and Biopython [including deep learning as well as machine learning]. Through field- and lab-based studies and computational tools, I have meticulously honed my skills through rigorous academic and informal coursework and immersive hands-on internships. My ardent ambition is to bridge the gap between farmers' fields and research stations through innovation exchange and capacity building. As a guiding principle, I am deeply committed to addressing the pressing food and nutrition insecurity issue in developing countries. Moreover, I am eager to collaborate on impactful projects that make a positive difference by bridging the gap between research stations and farmers' fields through capacity building as well as innovation exchange.

Projects

  • Project 1: Study of climate-smart practices - This project focuses on climate-smart practices, green jobs, and green enterprises at Lumbini Saskiritk Municipality and Samarimai Rural Municipality.
  • Project 2: Multivariate selection of Maize Hybrids - A project focused on selection of prime-yielding and Stable genotypes
  • Project 3: Screening of 761+ wheat lines and 62 released wheat varieties - Selection of resistance genotypes against spot blotch and leaf rust on local races
  • Project 4: Deep learning for Crop Improvement - Training of 10k+ plant leaf samples to develop a UAV vehicle for real-time solution

Assignments
