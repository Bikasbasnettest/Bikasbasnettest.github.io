Google Scholar ORCID

I am an enthusiastic graduate student in agriculture with avidity for not only crop improvement (genomics, plant-microbe interactions, quantitative genetics, applied aspects of plant breeding, modern plant breeding tools, product design and development), but also computational genetics programming and Biopython [including deep learning as well as machine learning]. Through field- and lab-based studies and computational tools, I have meticulously honed my skills through rigorous academic and informal coursework and immersive hands-on internships. I aspire to bridge the gap between farmers' fields and research stations through innovation exchange and capacity building. As a guiding principle, I am deeply committed to addressing the pressing food and nutrition insecurity issue in developing countries. Moreover, I am eager to collaborate on impactful projects that make a positive difference by bridging the gap between research stations and farmers' fields through capacity building and innovation exchange.

Projects

  • Project 1: Study of climate-smart practices - This project focuses on climate-smart practices, green jobs, and green enterprises at Lumbini Saskiritk Municipality and Samarimai Rural Municipality.
  • Project 2: Multivariate selection of Maize Hybrids - A project focused on selection of prime-yielding and Stable genotypes
  • Project 3: Screening of 761+ wheat lines and 62 released wheat varieties - Selection of resistance genotypes against spot blotch and leaf rust on local races
  • Project 4: Deep learning for Crop Improvement - Training of 10k+ plant leaf samples to develop a UAV vehicle for real-time solution

Assignments

  • A1: Agricultural Impact Analysis - Analyzing the environmental impacts of various agricultural practices through a comparative study.
  • A2: Crop Modeling - Creating models to predict crop yield based on climatic data and soil conditions.
